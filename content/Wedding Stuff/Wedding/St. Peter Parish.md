# Parish Details
>[!warning] "Please make an appointment to meet with the Pastor in preparation for your wedding at least <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">6 months in advance</mark> of your wedding date. It is important that you meet with the Pastor before sending out wedding invitations."
#### [Intro Letter from FR Felino](https://www.stpeterparish.net/weddings-fr-felino-letter)
- The pastor expresses joy and support for couples preparing for marriage, emphasizing that their wedding is significant not only for them but also for their families, the parish, and the wider Catholic community.
- He highlights that choosing a Catholic wedding involves embracing the Church’s expectations for the ceremony, which are intended to give the event deep Christian meaning rather than simply being social traditions.
- The parish staff and priests are committed to assisting couples in planning a beautiful and faith-filled wedding, encouraging careful preparation and inviting couples to reach out for guidance as they move forward.
- Please call me at <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">(206) 722-7888, ext. 119</mark> after you have read the enclosed guidelines for an appointment to proceed with your marriage preparation.

#### [Wedding Guidelines](https://www.stpeterparish.net/wedding-guidelines) 
- No professional staff other than the priest (all others are volunteers)
- No pets allowed
- No alcohol allowed on premesis
- Pictures are done before wedding starts

#### [Required Documents](https://www.stpeterparish.net/wedding-required-documents) 
- Baptism Certificate
	- Contact the church where you were baptized. Tell the church that you are getting married, and need an updated certificate. They will need to know the year of your baptism. The pastor there will send out a certificate. The date the church issues your baptism certificate should be within the last six months.
- Witness Statements
	- In the Archdiocese of Seattle, these bear the numbers “29.1” and “29.2”.
- Marriage License
	- Usually obtained about a month before the wedding. 
		- There are expiration dates and blackout periods so be aware
- Marriage Prep Certificate (Classes)
	- Catholic Life Ministries facilitated
		- <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">$175 per couple</mark>
	- please contact Robert Fontana at 509-731-6012
	- FOCCUS Inventory
		- <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">$35 per couple</mark>
		- Completed on the first session with the priest
		- 1.5 hours in duration
- Donation for the church
	- <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">$855.00 for non-parishioners</mark>
- [Wedding Church Usage Form](https://www.stpeterparish.net/wedding-church-usage)
